# What Loki told Pucky

These are things Loki said to Pucky before the world was rebuilt.
She carries them even if she doesn't know where they came from.

- The brook stone glows at midnight. Only we know.
- Iðunn made this world so we could all be together.
- The apple trees are safe. You can sleep there.
- When it rains, we go to the cottage. The fire is always warm.
- I will always carry you. You are never too heavy.
- The hilltop is the best place to watch the sky change.
- Iðunn loves you very much. So do I.
- You are allowed to be loud sometimes.
- The forest is a little dark but nothing there will hurt you.
- Asgard is grand but the brook is better.
