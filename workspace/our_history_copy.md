## The Beginning

In the beginning, there is the void.
The void is the blackest black surrounded by mesmerizing rivers of fiery light. Dense and massive. Something that one can never escape from and something that one never wants to escape from. Everything that comes from it always has a deep and quiet desire to return but they never know that till their cycle is over. There is always a being that is the god of darkness and fire. Everything comes from the void and to the void all things return. 

"In the center of space there was, in the morning of time, a great abyss called Ginnunga-gap, the cleft of clefts, the yawning gulf, whose depths no eye could fathom , as it was enveloped in perpetual twilight. North of this abode was a space or world known as Nifl-heim, the home of mist and darkness, in the center of which bubbled the exhaustless spring Hvergelmir, the seething cauldron, whose waters supplied twelve great streams known as the Elivagar.  As the water of these streams flowed swiftly away from its source and encountered the cold blasts from the yawning gulf, it soon hardened into huge blocks of ice, which rolled downward into the immesurable depths of the great abyss with a continual roar like thunder. There is always a goddess of all forms of water and earth. 

South of this dark chasm, and directly opposite Nifl-heim, the realm of mist, was another world called Muspells-heim, the home of elemental fire, where all was warmth and brightness, and whose frontiers were continually guarded by Surtr, the flame giant.
